The Assistant System

Six assistants. Every phase of a file.

Each assistant works alongside your team for a specific phase of the title file. They handle the coordination, communication, and follow-up so your staff can focus on the work that takes real expertise.

Phase 1 · Origination
Order Intake Assistant
Processes incoming orders from any channel so your staff does not have to re-enter data by hand. It extracts party and property details, flags anything incomplete, and creates the file ready to work.
Phase 2 · Discovery
Search Coordination Assistant
Handles vendor selection, dispatch, and follow-up so your team never has to track down a search. It monitors turnaround times against agreed deadlines and escalates before anything goes past due.
Phase 3 · Analysis
Abstract Reader Assistant
Reviews the returned search package and reads the title chain so your examiner does not have to start from scratch. It prepares a clear summary of what was found and flags every item that needs attention.
Phase 4 · Clearance
Clearance Assistant
Tracks every outstanding exception alongside your team. It sends follow-up requests for payoffs and estoppels on schedule and prepares the commitment draft when the file is ready, so nothing sits waiting.
Phase 5 · Closing
Closing Preparation Assistant
Manages the coordination behind every closing: scheduling, confirmations, wire instruction tracking, and package assembly. Your closer arrives prepared instead of spending the morning chasing down details.
Phase 6 · Recording
Recording & Post-Close Assistant
Handles recording submissions, tracks receipts from the recording office, and processes disbursements per the settlement statement. Post-close stops being a bottleneck and your policy goes out on time.
How It Works

Order in. Policy out.

Your assistants work continuously across every open file. Your team does not coordinate the process. They direct it.

1

Order Comes In

An order arrives by email, portal, or lender API. Your intake assistant pulls out every key detail, flags what is missing, and creates the file so your team can start immediately without re-entering a thing.

2

Your Assistants Get to Work

From search dispatch through closing preparation and recording, each assistant handles the coordination and follow-up for their phase. Your team stays in the loop without managing every touchpoint manually.

3

Your Team Focuses on What Matters

Your staff spends time on the decisions that require real expertise, not on chasing vendors or drafting status updates. Every file moves forward. Nothing falls through the cracks.
